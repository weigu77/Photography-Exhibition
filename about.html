About: Thank you for exploring the story exhibition of Chinese and Western architectural heritage! We hope that your journey through architectural styles, cultural comparisons, historical heritage and sustainable design will enrich your understanding of the diverse architectural landscape around the world. Our goal is to spark curiosity, foster dialogue, and build deeper connections through the universal language of architecture. Continue to see, explore, and savor the stories behind each building.
