Photography Exhibition of Chinese and Western Architectural Cultural Heritage Stories

This exhibition adopts the innovative narrative framework of "Architectural Image Dialogue Across Time and Space" as its central theme, meticulously selecting representative architectural images. It commences with an exploration of the grandeur of ancient Chinese capitals, revealing their millennia-old construction wisdom. The symmetrical design of the Forbidden City, with every intricate detail, exemplifies the ingenuity of ancient architects. Subsequently, it delves into the architectural genetic code of Jin merchants' civilization, emphasizing the illustrious history and distinctive commercial acumen of Shanxi Province. Concurrently, it introduces the quintessence of British architectural aesthetics, juxtaposing it with Chinese architectural characteristics for comparative analysis. Through five progressively structured chapters, the exhibition thoroughly examines the diverse interpretations of timeless beauty by various civilizations, offering audiences a profoundly moving journey through the realm of architectural aesthetics.
